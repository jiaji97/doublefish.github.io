Double Fish Technology is a multi-faceted development group integrating catering, real estate, and online games. Since its establishment in 2017, the group is based in the Philippines Based in Manila, with a capital of 900,000 RMB and 8 employees, it has grown to a capital of 600 million RMB and has nearly 500 employees. A multi-directional group with offices in many Asian countries. The two founders of the group are both post-90s graduates from Fudan University. Adhering to the concept of "Don't forget the original intention and have the courage to break through", they have created a Vibrant, brave and innovative corporate culture. The group aims to provide overseas Chinese with a high-quality and convenient living environment, starting from the basic necessities of life, Huge commercial breakthroughs have been made in Manila, Philippines and Bangkok, Thailand, and are well received by local overseas Chinese in the fields of catering and real estate. Looking to the future, the backbone of the former Tencent game development team is the core figure, and an office center is set up in Dubai, the economic center of the United Arab Emirates. Actively enter the video game market.

Adhering to the idea of daring to break through, one of the founders of the group led a team to the UAE to set up an office, and began to recruit technical talents from various countries to develop game programs. The first game program "FISHING DIARY" developed by the team successfully attracted the famous game operator DROIDHEN GAMES to operate. Since its launch, it has accumulated up to 10 million overseas downloads.
